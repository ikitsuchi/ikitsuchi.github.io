Magic Shop of Raspberry is the unused stage 5 theme of Touhou Gensoukyou ~ Lotus Land Story (東方幻想郷　～ Lotus Land Story), which was released at Comiket 54 in the summer of 1998. In this game, Marisa Kirisame returns as a playable character for the second time, and the powerful youkai Yuuka Kazami makes her first appearance.
